About

I have about 10 years of experience in the data science and AI industry, working across diverse domains including NLP, time series forecasting, large language models (LLMs), AI agents, and business intelligence. Currently, I am pursuing a PhD and teaching the course Artificial Intelligence at the Ukrainian Catholic University. My research interests focus on persuasive speech and the influence of technology on society.
