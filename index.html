About

I am a Research Scientist working at the intersection of artificial intelligence, natural language processing, and social science. With more than 10 years of experience in the data science industry, I bridge academic research with real-world AI systems.

My research focuses on computational propaganda, information manipulation, and autonomous AI agents. I study how modern AI technologies interact with information ecosystems and influence public discourse.

In parallel with research, I lead AI and R&D teams and consult companies on applied machine learning systems. I also mentor student research projects, several of which have led to publications at international workshops and conferences.
